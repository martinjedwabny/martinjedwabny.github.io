Hey there!

I am Martin Jedwabny and I am a PhD. student at the University of Montpellier. I am also a teaching assistant for the first year course “Imperative Programming” and fourth year course “Logic”. Before, I was a Masters student at University of Buenos Aires. There, I was a teaching assistant for the fourth year course “Programming Languages Paradigms”.

Research interests

My research interests are varied and include Logic, Non-monotonic reasoning, Non-classical logics, Argumentation, Probability, Machine Learning and Neural Networks. You can also check out my blog to see what I am working on.

Affiliations
